Andrew Gavin

Andrew Gavin is a NJ based musician and composer. Recently graduated from Rowan University, Andrew is eager to take on the world and bring his unique style and vision to the world of composition. A talented singer and bass player as well, Andrew has performed all across the State of NJ and Pennsylvania. Let him help you make your next musical experience one you’ll never forget!

Andrew Gavin has been surrounded by music his whole life. While a child, he was constantly being dragged to rehearsals, shows, lessons, and conferences by his mother, who was a bass player. Coming from a large musical family helped nurture his love for music as well. Every family gathering was filled with music making and merriment, which helped shape Andrew’s desire to be a professional musician.

A graduate of Rowan University, Andrew holds a Bachelor of Music degree in Composition as well as a Bachelor of Arts in Voice. While at Rowan, Andrew studied various aspects of music from composition with Bruce Yurko and Roberto Pace, Voice with Lauren Athey-Janka and Brian Ming Chu, and double bass with Douglas Mapp.

Andrew’s music combines the best in modern composition styles. His lush melodies are constantly being reworked and twisted during his compositions. He never fails to surprise and delight the listener. Andrew’s compositions capture varying aspects of the mind and he can create vivid and stunning images through his compositions. Andrew has written for a variety of ensembles in the classical world, as well as unconventional ensembles and groups. Andrew can write for any combinations of instruments at your request

Andrew’s singing has always been heralded with great reviews. His many singing credits include the Philadelphia Symphonic Choir, cantoring jobs at various churches including St. Andrew’s in Gibbsboro, NJ, and multiple concerts and recitals.

Andrew is also a freelance bass player having played in orchestras and in pits for both operas and musicals. He is based out of Central NJ.
